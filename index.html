The BIU Agents Hackathon is a 24-hour event tailored for CS students from 2nd year and above and Engineering students from 3rd year and above (undergrads and MSc students). Participants will prototype, code, and develop agents using LLMs as their core technology — while enjoying intensive hacking, networking, food, and the chance to win prizes!

FAQ

Who can participate?

CS students from 2nd year and above, and Engineering students from 3rd year and above (undergrads and MSc).

When and where?

March 29–30, BIU CS Building, common area (ground floor). The hackathon runs for 24 hours.

What should we build?

Anything agent-based using LLMs as a core technology. You can also pick from proposed projects by PhD students.

Do I need a team before registering?

No. You can register solo and form a team on-site during the kickoff. Ideal team size is 3–4 students.

Will food be provided?

Yes! Food and drinks will be available throughout the 24-hour event.
